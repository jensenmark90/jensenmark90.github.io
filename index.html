About Me

I'm Mark Jensen and I currently reside in Naperville, IL. I work in the telecommunications industry as a GIS Analyst generating proposed fiber optic network maps for cost estimating purposes to our vendors, as well as digitizing engineered drawings of company asset maps and maintaining asset GIS data.

Currently, I am a master’s student at the University of Wisconsin – Madison studying GIS & Web Map Programming. I have 5+ years of full-time work experience in the GIS industry, previously as a cartographer for a land plats mapping company and various local government internships.

Aside from my professional role, I am an avid traveler. As a hobby I traveled to every county in Illinois and Wisconsin and assembled award-winning maps of my county travels. I’m currently working toward the goal of visiting all 50 states and creating a nation-wide “selfie” map of every state welcome sign. I am up to 48 of 50 states as of August 2018.

My array of GIS skills is eclectic and broad. Let me assist you in solving your geospatial and cartographic needs!
